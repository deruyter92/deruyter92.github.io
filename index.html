    My research project is focused on deep learning-based optimization of visual prosthetics for the blind. Our research contributes to the development of a neuroprosthetic implant that can restore an elementary form of vision in the blind. In particular, we explore methods for improving the image quality for the user using artificial intelligence. Through virtual prototyping with simulation studies, we hope to improve the functional quality in everyday activities of daily living.

    The development of visual prosthetics is expected to support the independence and quality of life of millions of people with impaired vision. Besides this clinical goal, our research aims to advance our understanding of the human brain and artificial intelligence.
